Club events

Here at the BU Cycling club we many events weekly to cater to all our members. Some of these events include:

  • Racing Wednesdays
  • Serious Sundays
  • Lazy Rides

Racing Wednesdays

Take part in several races on wednesday ranging from quick sprints to full cycle races around the whole campus. There are events for people just having fun as well as serious professional races. Races begin from 4pm to 7pm every wednesday. Meet up is at main field.

Serious Sundays

Sundays is our weekly league races where people compete in a variety of cycling events to gain as many points as possible. Points are obtained via finishing in the top 3 in each race. Prizes ranging from vouchers to cash prizes. Activities happens every Sunday from 2pm - 6pm. Signup is required to participate.

Monthly Event: Lazy Rides

Every month cycle club members get together to cycle all across bournemouth beach, around the city centre and all back to the uni. This event is perfect for new members as everyone gets together for a leisurely cycle ride enjoying the view and having fun together. Meet up is at bournemouth pier at 1pm. Next event 29/10/2016.
